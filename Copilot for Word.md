
# 🧪 Lab — Create a Business Proposal in Word

## 🎯 Goal
Use **Word Copilot** to generate a professional business proposal.

---

## 🔧 Steps

1. Open **Microsoft Word** → New blank document  
2. Click **Copilot** (Home tab)  

3. Enter:
```
Create a business proposal to implement Microsoft 365 Copilot in our organization. Include:
- Executive Summary
- Business Benefits
- Implementation Approach
- Risks and Mitigations
Use professional business language.
```
4. Review the generated proposal  

5. (Optional) Refine with follow-up prompts:
You can improve the document with follow-up prompts such as:

- Add measurable KPIs for success tracking
```
Add measurable KPIs to evaluate success. 
```
- Convert risks into a table:
```
Expand this into a table with columns: Risk, Likelihood, Mitigation.
```
---

## ✅ Checkpoint

- Document includes all sections:
- Executive Summary  
- Business Benefits  
- Implementation Approach  
- Risks and Mitigations  
- Professionally written and formatted  

---

## 💡 Key Takeaway
**Word Copilot creates structured, ready-to-use business documents directly in your file.**
