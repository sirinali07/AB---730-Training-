# 🧪 Lab — Use Copilot in Outlook for Email Productivity 

## 🎯 Goal
Use **Outlook Copilot** to draft, summarize, and improve email communication.

---

## 📂 Step 1: Open Outlook

1. Open **Microsoft Outlook** (Desktop or Web)  
2. Go to your **Inbox**  

---

## ✉️ Step 2: Draft a New Email Using Copilot

1. Click **New Mail**  
2. Click **Copilot** → Select **Draft with Copilot**  

3. Enter the prompt:
```
Write a professional email to inform employees about the rollout of Microsoft 365 Copilot. Include benefits and next steps.
```

4. Review the generated email  

---

## ✍️ Step 3: Refine the Email

Use built-in options like:
- **Make it shorter**
- **Make it more formal**
- **Adjust tone**

Or enter a prompt:
```
Rewrite this email for an executive audience.
```

---

## 📩 Step 4: Summarize an Email Thread

1. Open an existing email conversation  
2. Click **Copilot → Summarize**  

✅ Copilot will generate:
- Key points  
- Decisions made  
- Action items  

---

## ✅ Step 5: Extract Action Items

```
List all action items from this email thread.
```


---

## 📅 Step 6: Generate a Reply

1. Click **Reply**  
2. Use Copilot:
```
Draft a response confirming next steps and timelines.
```

---

## ✅ Checkpoint

- Drafted a **professional email** using Copilot  
- Refined tone and clarity  
- Summarized an email thread  
- Extracted **action items**  
- Generated a **reply**  

---

## 💡 Key Takeaway

**Outlook Copilot helps you communicate faster, summarize conversations, and stay on top of actions—without manual effort.**
